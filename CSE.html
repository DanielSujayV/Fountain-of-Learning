KEY CONCEPTS TO LEARN:

  • COMPUTER ARCHITECTURE

    • Computer architecture is the science of designing and implementing the structure and functionality of a computer system. It includes the design of the computer's components, such as the CPU, memory, and I/O subsystems, and how they communicate with each other.

  • VLSI

    • Learning VLSI (Very Large Scale Integration) is essential because it’s the technology that enables the design and production of microchips, the "brains" of all electronic devices—from smartphones to medical equipment. Understanding VLSI helps in grasping how modern electronics are made smaller, faster, and more efficient, impacting nearly every tech-based aspect of our lives.

  • Cybersecurity

    • Learning cybersecurity basics is crucial for everyone, as it helps protect personal information, financial assets, and privacy online. In an increasingly digital world, understanding how to stay safe online can prevent identity theft, fraud, and cyberattacks that can impact anyone, regardless of technical background.

  • Embedded systems

    • Embedded systems are small, specialized computers within larger devices that help them perform specific tasks efficiently. In electronics and communication, they enable devices like smartphones, medical equipment, and home appliances to function intelligently, connecting and automating our everyday technology.
