KEY CONCEPTS TO LEARN:

  • Operating systems

    • Learning about operating systems is essential in computer science and design because it reveals how software interacts with hardware, manages resources, and supports multitasking. This foundation allows designers and developers to create efficient, compatible, and user-friendly applications.

  • Cybersecurity

    • Learning cybersecurity basics is crucial for everyone, as it helps protect personal information, financial assets, and privacy online. In an increasingly digital world, understanding how to stay safe online can prevent identity theft, fraud, and cyberattacks that can impact anyone, regardless of technical background.

  • Cloud computing

    • Learning cloud computing is essential in Computer Science and Design because it enables easy access to vast computing resources, storage, and tools over the internet. This flexibility supports powerful applications, collaboration, and creativity without needing expensive hardware, making it ideal for both technical and creative projects.
